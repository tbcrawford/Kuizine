About

Kuizine is a cross-platform mobile application for Android and iOS. It aggregates all of the restaurant options within the town of Kutztown, PA and provides relevant information including menu, contact information, and address of a restaurant in an intuitive, easy to use way. The application also provides logged in users the ability to save their favorite restaurant for ease of access later.
